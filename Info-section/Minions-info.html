Minions: The Rise of Gru

About
Growing up in the suburbs in the middle of the 1970s, among a flurry of flared jeans and feathered hair, is Gru (Steve Carell). Gru, a fan of the Vicious 6, a supervillain superteam, devises a scheme to get bad enough to infiltrate them. Fortunately, he has some mayhem-creating support from his devoted minions. Kevin, Stuart, Bob, and Otto, a new Minion with braces and a strong desire to please, work together to put their abilities to use as they and Gru construct their first hideout, test out their first arsenal, and complete their first tasks.Gru holds an interview to join the Vicious 6 after they remove their commander, famed fighter Wild Knuckles (Alan Arkin).
