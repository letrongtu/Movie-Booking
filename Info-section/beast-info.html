Beast

About
The whisper in the bushes can occasionally be a monster. In a suspenseful new thriller starring Idris Elba (Fast & Furious Presents: Hobbs & Shaw, The Suicide Squad), a man and his two teenage children are pursued by a large renegade lion who wants to show that the savannah has only one apex predator. Elba portrays Dr Nate Daniels, a newly bereaved father who travels to South Africa with his kids on a long-planned trip to a game reserve run by Martin Battles, an old family friend and wildlife expert (Sharlto Copley, Russian Doll series, Maleficent).
