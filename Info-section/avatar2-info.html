Avatar: The Way of Water 2

About
Avatar: The Way of Water opens with the narrative of the Sully family, the difficulty that haunts them, the lengths they take to keep each other safe, the fights they fight to stay alive, and the losses they experience. It is set more than ten years after the first movie's events. Jake Sully resides on the planet of Pandora with his newly established family. Jake must cooperate with Neytiri and the Na'vi race's army to defend their world as a recurring danger returns to complete what it started.
